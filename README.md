#  Beyond the Window

## Immersive VR Experience

Beyond the Window is a short immersive VR experience inspired by *2001: A Space Odyssey*.

The project places the audience inside a futuristic space station where they become the astronaut. Through interaction with an AI system and observation of distant planets, the experience explores themes of isolation, memory, artificial intelligence, silence, and human existence in space.

Rather than focusing on gameplay, the project emphasizes atmosphere, cinematic visuals, spatial storytelling, and emotional immersion.

---

#  Concept

Inside the station, users navigate a minimal futuristic environment guided by an AI voice system.

Through a large observation window, they witness planets, stars, and abstract cosmic movement while the AI communicates philosophical and emotional reflections about humanity and technology.

The project combines:
- VR immersion
- AI interaction
- environmental storytelling
- cinematic sound design
- contemplative exploration
